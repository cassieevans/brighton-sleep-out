What It’s All About

One night can make a real difference. Sleep out in solidarity with people facing homelessness, enjoy a powerful evening of live entertainment, and raise vital funds for Sussex Homeless Support. Homelessness is rising. Funding is falling. The need has never been greater. Every ticket, every sponsorship, and every person who sleeps out helps save lives on our streets.

First and foremost, I want to say a huge thank you to everyone who has supported Sussex Homeless Support over the years. Your support is what keeps us going.
SHS is now in its 11th year, and sadly the number of people experiencing homelessness continues to rise, with no clear solution in sight. The cost of living has doubled in many areas, while our funding has fallen by around 27% over the last two years. We’ve already cut everything we can without affecting frontline services — and now, quite simply, we need to find new funds to keep helping the people who rely on us.
In December 2019, we took part in the World Big Sleep Out. Despite one of the worst storms of the year, people showed up in huge numbers. The funds raised supported our work for over three years, helping carry us through COVID and beyond. That generosity made a real, lasting difference. Now, six years on, it’s time to do it again. On Sunday 18 January 2026, we’re coming together for another Sleep Out in Brighton. With incredible entertainment, strong community backing, and renewed urgency, this event has the power to change lives — and I truly hope you’ll be part of it. Thank you, as always, for standing with us.

— Jim Deans, Sussex Homeless Support

FAQ's

  • What to Bring?

    Please bring warm, layered clothing, a sleeping bag, and something to sleep on (such as a ground mat or cardboard). Comfortable shoes, a small bag, and a fully charged phone are recommended. Hot food will be available overnight, with breakfast, tea, and coffee served from 7am.

  • Where is it hosted?

    We will be throwing the sleep out at the Volks Bar and Venue. 3 The Colonnade, Madeira Drive, Brighton, BN2 1PS

  • Can I donate instead of taking part?

    Of course, we welcome donations. You can donate over on our enthuse page. Thank you.
